Strawberry Shortcake

Home Strawberry Cheesecake Strawberry Matcha

Description

Skip the store-bought and make summertime strawberry shortcake completely from scratch with this recipe! These lightly sweetened biscuits are simply perfect for holding juicy strawberries and fresh whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Sugar
  • Cold and cubed butter
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Cold heavy cream or Heavy whipping cream
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

Strawberry Filling

  1. Chop up the strawberries and mix with a little sugar.
  2. I recommend mixing the strawberries and sugar together before you make the biscuits; this gives the strawberries some time to juice up.

Biscuits

  1. First, pour the crumbly dough onto a floured work surface:
  2. Flatten the dough to about 3/4 inch thick with your hands or use a rolling pin:
  3. Fold both ends into the center:
  4. Turn it:
  5. Flatten again and repeat 2 more times.
  6. Use a biscuit cutter to shape them. You can use a 3-inch biscuit cutter or 2.75-inch biscuit cutter. You can bake the biscuits on a lined baking sheet, cast iron skillet, or round cake pan. A brush of heavy cream and sprinkle of coarse sugar gives the sweet biscuits a delightfully crisp top.

Whipped Cream

  1. Whip the heavy whipping cream or heavy cream with sugar. You can control how sweet you want it to be.
